Project maturity assessments

Overview

Maturity assessments for the Study App repository — two complementary streams under this index. The Engineering Maturity stream benchmarks 30 practices across 6 weighted axes against world-class engineering standards (Google SRE Book, GitLab Handbook, DORA, OWASP ASVS, CNCF). The Tech Docs Maturity stream benchmarks 144 practices across 12 weighted axes (v2) against top-tier IT documentation standards (Diátaxis, MADR 4.0, OpenAPI 3.1, Backstage, Stripe, GitLab Handbook, Mintlify). Each report scores the AS-IS situation with direct file-path evidence and tracks a prioritized gap workflow. The v2 edition adds two new axes (Onboarding & Contributor Experience; Trust, Compliance & Documentation Analytics) and URL-anchored benchmarks per row.

For the assessment template, file-naming rules, and the copy-paste LLM prompt, see AUDIT_TEMPLATE.html. Prompts used to generate the streams are at services/portal/internal/team/people/ivan-boyarkin/notes/maturity audit promt.txt (engineering) and services/portal/internal/team/people/ivan-boyarkin/notes/tech docs maturity audit promt.txt (tech docs).
