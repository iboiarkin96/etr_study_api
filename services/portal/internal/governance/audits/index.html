Architecture & quality assessments

Overview

Maturity assessments for this repo. Each report lists reference practices (Table 1), scores how we match them (Table 2) with a short justification per row, then scoring arithmetic, Top-5 gaps with workflow status, and next steps. ADRs record decisions; assessments record how we compare to common practice on a given date.

For the template, file-naming rules, required HTML structure, and the copy-paste LLM prompt, see the Assessment template.

Project Maturity

Project maturity assessments

Two complementary streams under one index. Engineering Maturity — 30 practices across 6 weighted axes benchmarked against Google SRE Book, DORA, OWASP ASVS, and CNCF Security Whitepaper. Tech Docs Maturity — 144 practices across 12 weighted axes (v2) benchmarked against Diátaxis, MADR 4.0, OpenAPI 3.1, Backstage, Stripe, GitLab Handbook, Mintlify, and the 2025–2026 AI-readability frontier (llms.txt, kapa.ai, Mintlify AI). v2 adds two new axes (Onboarding & Contributor Experience; Trust, Compliance & Documentation Analytics) with URL-anchored benchmarks per row.
