Tech radar — the stance on every technology we touch

Four rings, Zalando-style: Adopt (default choice), Trial (in real use, verdict pending), Assess (worth a spike, not in use), Hold (do not start new work with it). Every ring move that matters routes through the decision process — the radar records stances, ADRs record the reasoning.

Radar rules

  • The radar records stances, not reasoning. Any Adopt↔Hold move, and any Trial promotion, cites an ADR; the radar row links it.
  • Quarterly review. Each review either re-confirms a ring or moves the entry — an untouched radar is worse than none, because it reads as current.
  • Trial entries must name exit criteria. A trial without a promotion/demotion condition is an adoption in disguise.
