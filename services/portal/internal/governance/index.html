Governance

Overview

Per RFC 0004 Phase F, this hub consolidates the four governance trees that record what we decided, why, what was reviewed, and what is queued. They previously lived at services/portal/{adr,rfc,audit,backlog}/; ADR 0028's "outside services/" rule is satisfied because services/portal/ itself stays at repo root.

Sections

  • Architecture Decision Records — the what and why of every architectural decision (ADR 0001 → 0029).
  • Requests for Comments — implementation specifications and design rationale that an ADR intentionally omits (RFC 0001 → 0004).
  • Audit reports — dated architecture / quality / security assessments per ADR 0024.
  • Backlog — single source of truth for what is planned, in flight, blocked, and shipped.
