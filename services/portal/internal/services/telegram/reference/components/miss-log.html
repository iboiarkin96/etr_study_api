Component spec: MissLog

Append-only list of miss rows on the Errors screen — .tma-miss-log + .tma-miss-row. Read-only (no swipe, no delete); the tone dot warms with age (today = ember, yesterday = warn, 2+ days = rested / sage) so the shape of misses reads at a glance without demanding attention.

Overview

Each row carries a compact relative time chip («09:12», «yest», «3d»), a tone dot, the miss note, and — if the miss was linked to a conspectus — the conspectus title as a sub-line. Time labels are computed per-row client-side (cheap; ~10-40 rows per week). No pagination — the Errors surface is intentionally a weekly rhythm; older data is out of scope.

Age → tone rule

AgeToneTime chip
< 24 haccent (ember)«HH:MM»
1 daywarn«yest»
≥ 2 daysrested (sage)«Nd»

Warmth-of-recency instead of severity — misses aren't failures, they're noticed material. Today's ember reads «still working on this»; older sage reads «shape observed, now on the schedule».

Accessibility

  • Semantic list. <ol> — «newest first» is meaningful order, and AT enumerates «row 1 of N».
  • Tone dots decorative. The time chip carries the age; dots are aria-hidden.
