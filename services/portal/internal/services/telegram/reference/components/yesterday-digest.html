Component spec: YesterdayDigest

One-line recap of the previous day on Today. Renders on the .tma-digest primitive — icon + main copy — with an accuracy-driven tone that colours the mood at a glance.

Overview

Pure projection of the YesterdayDigest payload from GET /me/yesterday — reviewed / target / accuracy_pct / missed. The tone is derived client-side from accuracy_pct: ≥ 80 → success, ≥ 60 → warn, otherwise danger. Zero copy overrides — the i18n string carries the whole sentence; the component only supplies the token.

A zero-review yesterday still projects — the strip says «Reviewed 0 of N» in the danger tone, not «no data». The habit conversation belongs on every open, even a bad one.

Accessibility

  • Icon decorative. Tone is announced through the copy — «Reviewed 0 of 5» is unambiguous on its own; the icon is aria-hidden.
  • Tooltip on hover. The strip wears a .tma-tip — the tip label repeats the accuracy_pct for keyboard-first users on desktop.
