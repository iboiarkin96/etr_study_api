Component spec: MissOrb

The D1 «breathing element» of the Errors screen — the same warm orb primitive as StreakOrb, but the number is the seven-day miss count. State machine mirrors StreakOrb: rested (sage) when the count is 0, warm (ember) otherwise.

Overview

Renders the shared .tma-orb primitive with the weekly miss count in the centre and the localised «this week» cap below. Two states — rested at 0 (surface is sage: «you noticed everything on your first pass»), warm otherwise (ember: «noticed on purpose, on the schedule»). Every visual detail is inherited from the orb primitive — no bespoke sizing, no bespoke motion.
