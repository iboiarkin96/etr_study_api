Normative sources

The handbook is the working surface. The rules are ratified in these documents — read them when you need the rationale, not the procedure.

  • RFC 0003 — closed taxonomy of document types and their required sections.
  • ADR 0001 — docs live in the repository and ship through the same pipeline as code.
  • ADR 0018 — how ADRs are proposed, ratified, and badged.
  • ADR 0020 — PlantUML is the only diagram tool. C4 levels 1–3.
  • ADR 0026 — internal portal pages are the canonical version of every service's docs.
