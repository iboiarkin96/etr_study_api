How we write pages on this portal

The writers' handbook for everyone who edits documentation here. It answers two questions: how a page should be shaped (so the reader knows what kind of thing it is) and what should be inside it (so the reader actually gets the answer they came for). For specs that describe how the portal itself works — sidebar schema, page labels, catalog — see Portal reference. For the rules of the operating model — checks, lifecycle, where pages go — see Operating model.
