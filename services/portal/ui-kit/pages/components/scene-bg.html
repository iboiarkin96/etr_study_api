Scene background

Decorative layered radial-gradient mesh, used as an ambient backdrop on landings. Two intensities: default and soft.

Example · default

content sits on top

Variants

Soft

Public portal accent

Markup

<div class="docs-scene-bg" aria-hidden="true"></div>
<!-- soft variant -->
<div class="docs-scene-bg docs-scene-bg--soft" aria-hidden="true"></div>

Place inside a relatively-positioned container at z-index: 0; main content sits at z-index: 1 or higher.

States

Decorative — no interactive states.

A11y

  • Always set aria-hidden="true".
  • Ensure foreground text retains ≥ 4.5:1 contrast over the brightest mesh point.
  • Do not animate the gradient — it costs paint cycles and triggers motion-sensitive readers.

Tokens used

  • --grad-mesh-1, --grad-mesh-2, --grad-mesh-3
  • --bg (fallback)
