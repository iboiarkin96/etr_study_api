Purpose

Rendering misses (grouped by week, newest-first) makes the pattern legible — three misses in one week around distributed systems means «reset the schedule on that cluster». Deletion is deliberately absent: the log is append-only.

The point is not to feel bad about the miss. It's to notice the shape of the misses.
