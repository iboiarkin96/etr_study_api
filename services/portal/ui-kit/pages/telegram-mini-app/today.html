Purpose

Today serves two questions in ≤2 seconds: what do I do right now, and how did yesterday go. Even when nothing is due, the surface stays alive — streak ring + 90-day heat-map + Recently-reviewed keep the visual promise of daily consistency intact.

Today is the only screen the user actively opens. Everything else is a drill-down. Design it so cold-open feels like coming home.

States

Loading

Content-aware skeleton — digest + ring + 4 cell skeletons + shimmer heat-map.

Has due

All sections populated.

Nothing due

Due list swaps to «You've caught up. Retrieval is compounding — see you tomorrow.» Orb switches to data-state="rested" (sage), streak stays.

Streak broken

Digest turns rose-tinted. Encouragement to start now.

Milestone hit

Orb enters data-state="celebrate" — 900ms scale pulse + expanding ring halo, toast «Streak 30 · shield unlocked». Ambient hue-shift persists for the rest of the day.
