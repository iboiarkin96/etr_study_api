Screen DNA · living orb

Amie · signature
  • Emotional carrier beats editorial voice for a 2-second daily open. The breathing orb reads at a glance; a display digit + prose demands reading.
  • Streak state reads at a glance. Warm = active day, sage = rested, ember pulse = milestone — before the user reads any digit.
  • One CTA, editorial peek. «Start Focus» is the single primary action (D2); due list + recently reviewed render as quiet lined peeks (D3).
  • One accent, one rest state. Ember (progress) + sage (rest) is the whole semantic palette (D4).

01 · Today · Philosophy

Why Today matters

Today serves two questions in ≤2 seconds: what do I do right now, and how did yesterday go. Even when nothing is due, the surface stays alive — streak orb + 90-day heat-map + Recently reviewed keep the visual promise of daily consistency intact. Everything else in the app is either a drill-down or a nav-menu item — Today is the one screen the user actively opens.

Design it so cold-open feels like coming home.
