Purpose

The schedule is the antidote to «open the app · surprise: 15 due today». Show the shape of the next weeks so the user pre-empts spikes. Snoozing lives on Today / Conspectus, not here — this screen is a look-ahead.

You can't cram a spike after it hits. The schedule is the pre-emptive lever.

API endpoints

GET/api/v1/schedule/preview?days=90load projection GET/api/v1/conspectuses?due_on=YYYY-MM-DDday drill-down
