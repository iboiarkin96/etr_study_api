Screen DNA · living hello

Amie · signature
  • The orb greets the user first. The same .tma-orb primitive Today will carry — day-zero variant, digit «0», cap «day one». The user meets the app's signature identity before any prose is read.
  • One tagline, three moves, one CTA. The headline promises the outcome; the three E · T · R rows explain the method in one screen; the ember «Start today →» button lands the user on Today with the first due card visible.
  • The skip is a quiet ghost. A small tertiary-ink line under the primary CTA — available for the maintainer who reinstalls, invisible to the eye that scans headline → CTA.
  • Dismissed once, forever. onboarding_done lives in WebApp.CloudStorage with a localStorage mirror; Today's router gate redirects until it's set, and setting it again on a fresh device just re-shows the flow once (Telegram-native sync, no server column, no migration).

08 · Onboarding · Philosophy

Why Onboarding matters

Introduce the ETR method in a single screen and land the user on Today ready for their first review. The whole surface exists to answer «what is this app going to feel like every morning?» in one look — the orb is the answer before any prose is read.

First-run should feel like receiving a good gift — clear, personal, quick.
