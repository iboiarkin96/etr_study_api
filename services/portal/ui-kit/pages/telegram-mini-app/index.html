Philosophy · four rules

1 · Telegram-native, not portal

Cells, sections, and buttons match Telegram's iOS/Material clients. The portal UI Kit stays in the portal — the Mini App has its own .tma-* primitives.

2 · Theme follows the client

Every color reads from var(--tg-theme-*). Dark/light and custom themes flow in for free. Only the accent is fixed — that's study_app's identity.

3 · System font, no shipping fonts

SF Pro on iOS, Roboto on Android via the system stack. A webfont doubles FCP on cold LTE without a product win.

4 · Density is native, not dense

Cells are 44px minimum tap targets. Sections group 2–6 rows. Tables belong on the portal, not in the Mini App.
