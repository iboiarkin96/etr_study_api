Philosophy · four rules

Four architectural principles the Mini App design ships to. Not about the portal — about the mobile client.

01

Mini App speaks Telegram

Not a portal copy. Cells, sections, and buttons match Telegram's iOS/Material clients — the mobile client ships its own .tma-* primitives.

02

The client owns theming

Every colour reads from var(--tg-theme-*). When the user flips dark/light or picks a custom Telegram theme, the Mini App follows for free.

03

Zero shipping fonts

SF Pro on iOS, Roboto on Android — straight from the system stack. Loading a webfont doubles FCP on cold LTE for no product win.

04

Made for thumbs

Tap targets ≥ 44 px. Sections group 2–6 rows. Tables live on the portal — the Mini App never asks a thumb to do a mouse's work.
