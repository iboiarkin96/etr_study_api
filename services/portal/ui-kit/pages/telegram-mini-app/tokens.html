Tokens

Every color, size, spacing, and radius routes through a CSS custom property under the --tma-* namespace. Ad-hoc hex or px in the kit is a bug. The token file is assets_v2/ui-kit/components/tma-kit.css.

Colors

Palette maps to Telegram's themeParams. When the app runs inside Telegram, the client overwrites these variables. Fallbacks below emulate the iOS light theme.

Motion & haptics

Cheap and quick. Every animation is short enough that a slow device doesn't feel «laggy». Haptics fire on outcomes, not on opens.

InteractionMotionHaptic
Tap on a cell Background flash --tma-accent-soft, 120 ms selectionChanged
Primary button pressed Scale 0.98, 90 ms impactOccurred('light')
Mark reviewed → success Cell fades to success tint 200 ms; row collapses 240 ms notificationOccurred('success')
Log error saved Sheet slides down; «Logged» toast 1.5 s notificationOccurred('success')
Delete / destructive Row shakes 6 px, 180 ms, before confirm notificationOccurred('warning')
Pull-to-refresh Native platform (delegated to Telegram) impactOccurred('medium') on trigger

Motion respects prefers-reduced-motion — replace scale + fade with a straight color-swap. Every haptic call lives in web/src/shared/haptics.ts; nothing else invokes the SDK directly.
